YouTube Media Downloader

A desktop application that downloads YouTube media data. This includes a simple YouTube video, only audio, a cut version of video and audio, A cut version meaning that the user may give a start and the end time for the video and only that part will be downloaded instead of complete audio or video. For example, the user gives start time as 2:30 and end time as 3:30 the media downloaded will be a one-minute video/audio from 2:30 to 3:30.
